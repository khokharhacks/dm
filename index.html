ℹ️ About T2

Technical Tutor — built for Atlanta suburb students.

T2 (Technical Tutor) was founded to bridge the gap between students seeking quality tutoring and skilled educators in the Atlanta suburbs.

We serve high schoolers and university/college students with personalized, one-on-one tutoring — both online and in-person. Our unique ☕ Coffee & Connect program also offers informal mentoring over coffee for emotional support and career guidance.

📍 Initially serving: Alpharetta, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Roswell, Decatur, Dunwoody, Johns Creek, Smyrna, Kennesaw, Brookhaven & surrounding areas.
